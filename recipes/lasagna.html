Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a perfect comfy dish, that you can enjoy with your friends and family, or by yourself. You can also decide, if you want it to only be meaty lasagna, if you add some vegetables, or if you go for a vegetarian alternative.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • lasagna plates
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery stick
  • oregano

White sauce

  • 3 ss butter
  • 3 ss flour
  • 6 dl milk
  • 5 ss cheese
  • 1 ts salt
  • pepper
  • nutmeg

Steps

  1. Fry the meat in a pot
  2. Cut all the vegetables, fry them and add them to a pot with meat
  3. Add tomato sauce and tomato paste into the pot
  4. While meat sauce is cooking, start preparing the white sauce
  5. Melt butter in a new pot
  6. When the butter is melted, add flour and mix until smooth
  7. Add milk, mix and let cook for cca. 10 minutes
  8. Add cheese to the white sauce and let it melt.
  9. When everything is cooked, make a layer of lasagna plates, put meat sauce on top, then the white sauce and repeat, until you fill the whole baking pot.
  10. You can serve the lasagna with a garlic bread and salad on side.
