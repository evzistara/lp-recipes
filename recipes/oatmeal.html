Oatmeal

Description

If you want a healthy, filling and super easy breakfast, oatmeal bowl is the prefect recipe for you.

You only need five minutes, oatmeals, water and the rest is basically up to you.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup water
  • protein powder
  • almond milk
  • chia seads
  • peanut butter
  • banana
  • kesam
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Add oatmeal and water into a small bowl and warm in a microwave for one and a half minute.
  2. When it's done, it's time to add protein powder of your choice, fe. chocolate, chia seads and a bit of milk and mix.
  3. Cut banana into slices and put it on top. You can also use apples, blueberries or strawberries or combination.
  4. Add a spoon of kesam and a spoon of peanut butter.
  5. You're ready to enjoy your delicious breakfast"
