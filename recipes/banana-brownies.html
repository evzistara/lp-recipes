Banana brownies

Description

Do you love brownies, but would likea little healthier option? We got you. Try these banana brownies, that are extremely tasty, while having less sugar, than the usual ones.

These banana brownies are rich, fudgy and full of banana flavor. They taste like a cross between banana bread and brownies. As an added bonus, the batter can be easily prepared in the blender.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup mashed bananas
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cacao powder
  • 2-3 tbs honey
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chip
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat owen to 200 degrees C
  2. Mash bananas in a bowl.
  3. Add peanut butter and eggs and mix the ingredients until smooth and well combined.
  4. Add cacao powder, honey, baking soda and pinch of salt and mix well.
  5. Add 2/3 of chocolate chips and gently mix.
  6. Add the brownie batter to baking pan and put the rest of the chocolate chips on top.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
